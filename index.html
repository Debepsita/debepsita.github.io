About Me

I am a PhD student in Statistics and Data Science at The University of Texas at Austin. My research interests include causal inference, machine learning, Bayesian methodology, and statistical methods for complex and high-dimensional data.

I am broadly interested in developing principled statistical methods motivated by modern scientific, biomedical, and data-driven applications.

Research Interests

Causal Inference Machine Learning Causal Mediation Self-Distillation Bayesian Methods

My current research interests include causal mediation with functional data, modern machine learning methods, self-distillation in generative models, Bayesian methods, and statistical inference for complex data.
