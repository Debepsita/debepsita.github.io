About Me

I am a PhD student in Statistics and Data Science at The University of Texas at Austin. My research interests include causal inference, machine learning, self-distillation in generative models and statistical methods for complex and high-dimensional data.

I am broadly interested in developing principled statistical methods motivated by modern scientific, biomedical, and data-driven applications.

Research Interests

Causal Mediation Inference Machine Learning Self-Distillation

My current research interests include causal mediation with functional data, and self-distillation in generative models.
